### Reflection Question Response
In a blockchain-based bounty system, the commitment hashes and the finalized winner's address must be entirely public on-chain to ensure transparency and trust. Conversely, the plaintext answers and identifying participant details must stay hidden until the submission phase ends to prevent front-running and plagiarism. The AI's role should be focused on efficiently filtering submissions and conducting primary batch evaluations using automated LLM judging. However, the final critical decisions, such as resolving ambiguous edge cases and distributing rewards, should be handled by a human manager to maintain system integrity. Balancing these distinct responsibilities ensures that the judging mechanism remains both scalable and fair for all developers involved.
